About us

A home away from home!. It is a two storied building with spacious rooms for providing accommodation facility to 200 students and is designed to provide an ideal environment conducive for academic pursuit. The hostel is located in the north east side and adjacent to the college campus. Major facilities include dining halls, uninterrupted water supply, reading halls, internet connection, indoor recreation, television, hostel office, guest room and mess within the hostel premises. The mess has a centralized modern kitchen and dining room. Special menu will be introduced and served in the mess on all important religious festivals.
